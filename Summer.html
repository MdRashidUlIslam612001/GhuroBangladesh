    Summer In Bangladesh



    The summer season is the first season of the year. Summer is the hottest season of the year. It stays longer than another season. It generally starts in the month of March and continues up to June. The sun's rays are so strong. The temperature rises around 35 °c to 45°c. People become extremely exhausted in the scorching heat. The field undergoes cracking. Revers, ponds, lakes, etc get dried up. Birds and beasts pray for water. All nature looks dry and sunburnt. Many summer deaths are caused by dehydration. People take shelter in cool places. In the summer season people like to sleep in the open air at night. In summer we wear cotton light-colored clothes. In the summer we drink and eat cool things like ice cream, cold drinks, etc. Sometimes Strome with rain comes in the afternoon and it is called "Kalboishakhi". As a result, the heated earth gets temporary relief. Many festivals are observed in summer such as charak puja, Bengali new year, Rabindra Jayanti, etc. We get many fruits in this season like mango, watermelon, jackfruit pineapple, etc. Schools, colleges remain closed in this season. As a result, the students enjoy summer vacation.
